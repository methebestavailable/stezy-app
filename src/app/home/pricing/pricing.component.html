Pricing

With Stezy Blockchain, you pay for the Node you create. The Stezy managed Blockchain network consists of one or more partners, and each partner member has peer nodes with can communicate seamlessly with the consortium partners. Additionally, members can write data to the network. One Year Membership is absolutely free. You will only be charged for the peer nodes, peer node storage, data written to the network, and data transfer all consolidated as part of Subscription. Depending on the requirement you have the choice to select any of the plans. You can check our Feature comparison section. The pricing below is for Stezy Managed Blockchain for Ethereum and Quorum.
