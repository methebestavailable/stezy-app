1. Getting Started

We are glad to have you on board. To help you get started, we’ve prepared this series of guides for you. In this first guide, we’ll discuss an overview of what Stezy is and what you can do with it.

What is Stezy?

Stezy is a "Do it yourself Blockchain Platform", which helps companies, integrate and deploy blockchain solutions with ease by utilizing DIY network and smart contract builder on a fully automated & secured infrastructure.

What can you do with Stezy?

There are two key components which Stezy platform deals with . These are :

  • A blockchain network also commonly known consortium and
  • A blockchain code also commonly known as smart contract

By using Stezy platform, organizations can save time, cost and effort to setup blockchain networks, invite other participants to their network and deploy smart contracts.

Who uses Stezy?

The short answer is anyone with the intent to move towards decentralization and want to build greater level of trust and transparency into their existing system. In fact, Stezy can be used by:

  • Small & large businesses who wish to bring the blockchain advantage in their product and that too on a budget.
  • Small & large service companies who wish to serve their customers with blockchain solutions without requiring to hire costly and not so easily available blockchain developers and architects.
  • Developers & Freelancers who want to quickly get their hands dirty with blockchain technology and use them in their projects or just for fun.
How different is Stezy solution from other BaaS solutions?

Stezy is the only solution of its kind which provides most flexibility to the organisations in terms of choice of consensus protocols, frameworks and deployment regions.

Moreover, Stezy provides a smart contract builder which takes away the efforts of writing programs in solidity and manually deploying it on the blockchain node you own.
