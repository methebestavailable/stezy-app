Pricing

Billing is at the organization level while preserving visibility into usage in each project. Stezy charges theorganisations only for the blockchain nodes that are provisioned by them. Stezy charges monthly for your blockchain nodes. If organisations decide to cancel their ongoing node subscription, they will continue to use all the services tilltheir plan’s current expiry date is reached and their card won’t be charged for any upcoming months till they plan to start their subscription again.

There is no extra cost involved for using any of the smart contract builder services.

For all the plans, organisations have access to our complete API library for no extra cost involved.

Stezy currently is offering three plans : Starter, Standard and Enterprise. Organisations can choose the plan that best meet their needs.
